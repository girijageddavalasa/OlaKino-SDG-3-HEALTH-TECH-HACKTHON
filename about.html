About Our Application

Our application is designed with a primary goal to contribute towards achieving Sustainable Development Goal 3 (SDG 3) - Good Health and Well-being. We aim to facilitate healthier lifestyles by encouraging physical activities such as walking, jogging, and swimming among individuals and communities.

To learn more about SDG 3 and other Sustainable Development Goals, visit the official SDG 3 website.
